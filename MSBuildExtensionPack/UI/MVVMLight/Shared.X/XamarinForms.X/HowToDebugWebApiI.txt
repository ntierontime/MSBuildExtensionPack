1. Get SharpProxy from Github
https://github.com/jocull/SharpProxy
And run it according to:
http://www.codefromjames.com/wordpress/?p=97

2. Open ResourceApp.config

3. For Android, change AndroidWebApiRootUrl
to your local computer and the external port in SharpProxy

4. For IOs, change IOsWebApiRootUrl(same as #3 Android)
to your local computer and the external port in SharpProxy

5. For Windows, change WebApiRootUrl to localhost:<your asp.net mvc project port>
